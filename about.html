About

Impact Ballistics is built by shooters who wanted a fast, clear tool without the bloat. Focus: clean dope tables, reliable wind calls, and weather-aware adjustments. The goal is simple—reduce friction, increase hits.

Supports centerfire and rimfire workflows, one-decimal outputs in MIL or MOA, density altitude, optional spin drift, and PDF range cards. Runs on-device with minimal dependencies.
