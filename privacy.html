Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not sell your data. We do not run ad trackers, social pixels, or analytics that fingerprint devices.

Data The App Uses

  • Location (approximate): used only to find the nearest public weather station for temperature, pressure, humidity, wind, and elevation. Location data never leaves the device except as part of a one-off request to a weather provider.
  • Compass/Heading & Sensors: used locally to compute relative wind. Not stored or sent to servers.
  • Saved Profiles: rifles, ammo, and dope you enter are stored locally on your device.

Weather Provider Attribution

Weather data is obtained via the OpenWeather API under their free service tier. “OpenWeather” and “OpenWeatherMap” are trademarks of OpenWeather Ltd. Attribution is provided here and in the site footer to meet their usage requirements.

What We Send Over the Network

  • Requests to OpenWeather include coarse coordinates (or a station/city id) to return local conditions. No names, emails, or rifle/ballistics data are transmitted.

Your Choices

  • You can disable location permissions and enter weather manually.
  • Uninstalling the app removes your local data.
