Privacy Policy

Plain English. No surprises. Last updated: October 3, 2025

Overview

Impact Fuzion doesn’t collect, sell, or share your personal data. Your rifle profiles, dope tables, wind/DA inputs, and generated PDFs stay on your device. If you choose to share a file (e.g., email a dope card), that’s your choice and under your control.

Data We Collect

None. Impact Fuzion does not collect personal information or usage analytics.

On-Device Processing

All ballistic calculations, TRY/TRUE adjustments, and stored rifle/ammo profiles are processed locally on your device. The app works offline.

Optional Permissions

  • Location (Optional): Used for local weather/DA. Never leaves your device via Impact Fuzion.
  • Files/Storage: Only used when you export or save a PDF.
  • Network: Optional weather lookups or sending an email you initiate. No background tracking.

Third-Party Services

No analytics SDKs or advertising frameworks are embedded. External share sheets run under their own policies.

Children’s Privacy

Not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Security

We don’t host your data. Use device protections (passcode/Face ID) and keep your OS updated.

Your Choices

  • Use the app fully offline if you want.
  • Only export/share when you choose.
  • Revoke Location permissions anytime in Settings.
